How it all started!
One day while living in the quiet city of Sandusky, Ohio we decided that pizza was bland. The only options only being your standard run of the mill fast food pizza places and no real pizza joints, we decided to take matters into our hands and change that. Now 10 years later it's clear we are here to stay. The Sandusky area has championed us as best pizza place for 10 years in row since our opening. And we are a landmark for anyone passing through. If you've come to our town and are looking for food, look no further, it's pizza time.
